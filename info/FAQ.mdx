---

<Accordion title="What is BlastFi?">
  BlastFi is a comprehensive crypto platform that offers a range of features
  including staking, a dynamic leaderboard, an NFT viewer, a Bridge feature for
  asset transfers, and a Launchpad for participating in Initial DEX Offerings
  (IDOs).
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How does staking work on BlastFi?">
  BlastFi offers two staking options: flexible and locked. Flexible staking
  allows for withdrawal at any time, while locked staking involves locking
  assets for a specified period to earn higher yields and XP.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What is the BlastFi Bridge feature?">
  The Bridge feature enables seamless transitions of assets to the Blast chain,
  facilitating access to the platform's unique opportunities and functionalities
  within the BlastFi ecosystem.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I climb the BlastFi leaderboard?">
  Climbing the leaderboard is achieved through friend referrals, earning a
  percentage of the XP from those referrals, in addition to rewards from staking
  methods and bonuses.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I view and explore NFTs on BlastFi?">
  Yes, BlastFi includes an NFT viewer that allows users to explore and interact
  with their Blast chain NFTs directly within the platform.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Are there any features for users with lower budgets?">
  Yes, BlastFi ensures engagement and excitement for users with lower budgets
  through a diverse lottery system for IDO participation and by offering various
  staking options suitable for different investment sizes.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I get started with BlastFi?">
  To get started, create an account on the BlastFi platform, explore the
  available features like staking and the NFT viewer, and participate in
  community campaigns to begin your journey in the BlastFi ecosystem.
</Accordion>
